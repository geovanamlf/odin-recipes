Brigadeiro

Description

A classic Brazilian sweet made with condensed milk, butter, and chocolate powder. It’s soft, rich, and covered in chocolate sprinkles — perfect for any occasion

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 tablespoons of Nesquik (or cocoa powder)
  • Chocolate sprinkles (optional)

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat.
  2. Add the sweetened condensed milk and Nesquik, stirring constantly.
  3. Cook the mixture over low heat until it thickens and starts pulling away from the sides of the pan (about 10 minutes).
  4. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  5. Grease your hands with a bit of butter, roll the mixture into small balls, and coat them with chocolate sprinkles.
