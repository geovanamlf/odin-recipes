Bolognese Pasta

Description

A hearty pasta dish with flavorful ground beef in a rich tomato sauce, topped with cheese and baked to perfection. A simple, satisfying crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 300g of pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 300g of ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta with the Bolognese sauce.
  6. Serve on a plate and garnish with fresh basil or parsley and grated parmesan cheese if desired.
